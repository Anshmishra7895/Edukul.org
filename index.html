A Good Education is the foundation for a better future

Edukul works towards bridging the gap between underprivileged children and their education. Education empowers underprivileged children with skills and knowledge that makes them capable of building a bright future for themselves and their family.

Join Us

about us

An Initiative by TopTrove

Edukul, an initiative by the Top Trove foundation aims to provide basic education to 5 million underprivileged and poor children by 2025. We allow India's bright and promising young generation to volunteer to donate study material or serve as full-time teachers for underprivileged children in our society.
